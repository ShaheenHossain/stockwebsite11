Stock By Locations in Odoo

This app support product variant and product template warehouse location in Odoo if you get confused to see quantity of product for each location in your warehouse then,this app is really helpfull for you. This plugin will help you to see stock of each location on product level, you just need to open product-template/product-variant view, to see stock of all location of your warehouse, This application satisfies the desire to get all the states of stock quantities of particular product in a graphic by product's all internal locations.

Features

Stock report in graphic view

One graphic view of all the states is given on the product variant and the product template form where each line indicates internal location.

Gives view for on hand quantity.

You can have the idea of On Hand Quantity, Incoming ,Outgoing Quantity and Forecasted Quantity at glance on the table shown in the image here.

Gives calculated quantity

This quantities are derived by calculating all the quants and moves of the product.

Supports to all industry.
